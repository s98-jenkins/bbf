Become a Volunteer

The Baja Blues Fest Organization is seeking approximately 95 volunteers to assist with the 7th Annual Baja Blues Fest at the Rosarito Beach Hotel, Rosarito Beach in August – the opening evening of 17th (Friday), the day of 18th (Saturday) and the afternoon of 19th (Sunday) 2017.

Volunteers must read and agree to the Baja Blues Fest Volunteer Information Sheet.

To apply, please fill out the Volunteer Form and submit on-line if you wish to volunteer.  You will have an opportunity to review your entries before submitting.  Remember to click  PRINT so that it sends us the application.  You should get a Thank You Page from the Blues Fest if it was successful!

Deadline to apply is June 30, 2018.
